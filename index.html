Explore History

Everyone knows of some ancient civilizations. Ancient Rome, Ancient Greece, Ancient Egypt, all of these are covered again and again in our books, magazines, and television shows. But there are so many interesting, less well-known civilizations
Let's discover 4 other civilizations and learn about them.

  • The Khmer Empire

    The Khmer Empire (802 - 1431 CE) was one of the first organized political states in Southeast Asia. From its enormous capital at Angkor Thom, the kingdom held sway over territory that includes present-day Cambodia, Laos, Thailand, and southern Vietnam for nearly 500 years.

    Learn more about the Khmer Empire
  • The Nabatean Kingdom

    The Nabatean Kingdom was a powerful political entity which flourished in the region of modern-day Jordan between the 4th century BCE and c. 106 CE and is best known today for the ruins of its capital city of Petra. Nabateans were Arabian nomads who amassed their wealth first as traders.

    Learn more about the Nabatean Kingdom
  • The Ancient Shu Kingdom

    The ancient Shu Kingdom is a civilization dating back at least 4,800 years located in southwest China's Sichuan Province. The early Shu civilizationis represented by the Sanxingdui Ruins site. It is believed that the ancient Shu Kingdom lasted over 2,000 years.

    Learn more about the Ancient Shu Kingdom
